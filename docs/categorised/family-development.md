#### Window of Vulnerability for Mental Illness
The window of vulnerability for mental illness refers to specific periods in an individual's life when they are more susceptible to developing mental health disorders. This can be due to various factors such as biological changes, stress, or environmental influences, often occurring during adolescence or major life transitions.

#### Love Object Choice Psychology
This concept refers to the psychological processes involved in selecting a romantic or sexual partner. It is influenced by unconscious desires, past experiences, and internalized models of relationships. In psychoanalytic theory, love object choice is often linked to childhood relationships with parents or primary caregivers, shaping one's preferences and attractions in adulthood.
